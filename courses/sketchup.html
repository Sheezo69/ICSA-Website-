Program Overview

Master the basics of 3D modeling and rendering through SketchUp and prepare for creative design projects in architecture and interiors.

What You Will Learn

  • SketchUp interface and modeling tools
  • 3D object creation and scene setup
  • Layout planning and design presentation
  • Rendering workflow with V-Ray for SketchUp
  • Project-based architecture and interior visualization

Who Should Enroll

  • Interior and architecture beginners
  • AutoCAD learners adding 3D workflow
  • Freelancers in design and visualization
  • Students building practical design portfolios
